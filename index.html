Leavenworth, Washington

A Bavarian-style village in the Cascade Mountains, full of alpine views, seasonal festivals, and scenic trails along Icicle Creek.

Why I Love Leavenworth

Leavenworth is my favorite getaway in Washington. It’s a small town that feels like a trip to Europe—complete with alpine-style architecture, cozy coffee shops, and mountains all around. I love walking through Front Street surrounded by music and lights, visiting bakeries, and exploring trails that start right outside town. It’s the perfect mix of relaxation, culture, and nature.

Top Attractions

Front Street Park

The heart of Leavenworth—lined with alpine-style shops and restaurants, plus a central gazebo and year-round events. It’s especially magical in December when the Christmas lights cover every building. More info

Waterfront Park & Wenatchee River

Just a short walk from downtown, this peaceful park follows the Wenatchee River. It’s perfect for walks, picnics, and birdwatching. Park details

Leavenworth Reindeer Farm

A unique family-owned farm where visitors can meet reindeer up close! It’s fun year-round but especially festive during the holidays. Tickets & hours

Leavenworth Ski Hill

A classic small ski area right near town with tubing, cross-country skiing, and hiking in summer. Perfect for families and first-timers. More info

Quick Facts

  • Population: Around 2,400 people
  • Location: Central Washington, on U.S. Highway 2 in the Cascade Mountains
  • Vibe: Bavarian village with mountain views and European-style shops
  • Best seasons: Fall colors, winter lights, and summer river activities
  • Language: English (but lots of German words and songs!)
